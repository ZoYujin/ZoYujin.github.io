Professional Summary

I am a Ph.D. student working on reliable and efficient foundation models, with a focus on multimodal LLMs and vision–language models. My research studies how foundation models acquire, preserve, and use knowledge under real-world constraints such as compute, latency, and memory. Recent work spans memory-free continual adaptation, efficient hallucination mitigation, and post-training / reinforcement learning for general-purpose VLMs.
